American Diabetes Association		 Marcia Levine Mazur reports on singer Della Reese's career breakthrough--sitting at the Horn & Hardarts automat, so poor that she was making "ketchup soup" out of the free ketchup soup and hot water it offered, when she overheard a conversation at the next table that led to her first job as a singer.

Kalgoorlie Miner (Australia)		 Keren Holland reports than an unexpected Department for Community Development grant has sustained the existence of the Kalgoorlie-Boulder's Red Cross Soup Patrol, which feeds soup to some 8000 children and adults a year.
