Lemongrass

(Cymbopogon citratus)

Originating in India and Sri Lanka, this grass with a bulbous base is also known as oil grass, fever grass, and sereh. It is a favored herb in Southest Asia and remains the source of the lemony overtones of Asian cooking, from "citral," its oily lemon essence.
