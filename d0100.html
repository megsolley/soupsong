01/27/00
Reuters		 Olga Petrova reports from Sleptsovsk, Russia, that half a loaf of bread and enough soup to feed one hot meal per day for the over 200,000 refugees from Chechnya is grossly insufficient. She says, "the flame of the fire's last log is not enough to bring the pot of thin grey soup to the boil."

Reuters		 Ostrich producers are luring CBS personality David Letterman to become a spokesman for their product, saying that less-fatty ostrich meat made into "ostrich and tortellini soup" is just the thing for someone recovering from quintuple-bypass heart surgery.

Associated Press		 This just in: The FBI has published "A 'Classified' Cookbook from the FBI, 2nd edition," punctuated with the following soup recipes: Virginia Clam Chowder, Potato Leek Soup, Taco soup, Crab Soup, Turkey soup, Hangover soup, Bean Soup, Potato Soup, and Wild Rice Soup. What recipe did the wife of Director Louis Freeh contribute? Chicken Pillows.

Associated Press		 Yasser Arafat ended his visit to Washington, DC, by eating chicken soup at a traditional Friday night Shabbat dinner at the home of Washington hostess Esther Coopersmith, who has long promoted Arab-Israeli understanding.

Associated Press		 Laurie Kellman reports on presidential hopeful John McCain recalling his painful days as a POW in Vietnam...including hearing the footsteps of his captors, "coming to interrogate him or bring him a meal of rice, sometimes ruined with pebbles.
