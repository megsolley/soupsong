Cold Cruel Hearts of Palm Soup

Wildly decadent--and so delicate. Serve cold to 4 people and see if they can identify the key ingredient.
  • 2 Tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup celery and leaves, chopped
  • 3 cups vegetable or chicken stock
  • 1 14-oz. can of hearts of palm, chopped--reserving one heart
  • 1 cup whipping cream
  • 1 avocado
  • 4 paper thin slices of lemon
Melt the butter in a medium saucepan at low heat. Add celery and onion and cook until the onion is translucent. Add the stock and the chopped hearts of palm. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and cook over low heat for 30-40 minutes.

Puree the soup, solids first, then press through a sieve--extracting as much pulp as possible from the solids. Stir in the cream and the last heart of palm, chopped fine. Chill until very cold.

When ready to serve, ladle into bowls (packed in ice, if possible), and garnish each with one lemon slice and thin slices of avocado.
