Tomato Soup with Star Anise

Light, fresh, aromatic, and and palate stimulating; serve hot to 6 people as a first course.
  • 2 Tablespoons peanut oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, with leaves, chopped
  • 1 large carrot, chopped
  • 2 lbs. tomatoes, peeled and chopped, with juice
  • 4 cups vegetable or beef broth
  • 12 whole star anise
  • 2 sprigs parsley
Heat oil in a large saucepean over medium-low heat. Add onion and cook until translucent--about 10 minutes. Increase the heat to medium and add the celery and carrot. Cook until the vegetables begin to brown--about 5 minutes. Add tomatoes, juice, stock, 6 whole star anises, and parsley. Cover and simmer gently for 40 minutes.

Strain the soup through a fine sieve, pressing to extract the liquids. Degrease the soup, then return to a simmer. Ladle into bowls and garnish with the remaining star anise.
